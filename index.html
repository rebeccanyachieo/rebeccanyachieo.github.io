About

Both fields I work in are really about the same thing: reading a system under pressure, identifying what doesn't fit, and figuring out why. TCM does this through symptom patterns and diagnostic frameworks built over centuries. Security does it through logs, traffic, and behavioral baselines. The underlying skill transfers.

I'm self-directed in security — working through labs, building small tools, and documenting what I learn. I'm more interested in understanding how things work than in collecting certifications, though I'm working toward those too.
